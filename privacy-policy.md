# Privacy Policy for Fitly

*Last Updated: May 24, 2026*

## 1. Introduction

Fitly ("Fitly", "we", "us", or "our") operates in Australia and is committed to protecting your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose and protect personal information when you use the Fitly mobile application, website, platform and related services.

By accessing or using Fitly, you agree to the collection and handling of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### Personal Information

This may include:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Date of birth
* Profile information
* Suburb, location or service area information
* Trainer business details, such as ABN, if provided
* Trainer qualifications, certifications and insurance details, if provided
* Profile photos, service photos or other images uploaded by users
* Information shared through in-app messages, support emails or other communications with Fitly

### Age Verification

We may ask for your date of birth to verify that users are 18 years or older before using Fitly.

Date of birth is used for age verification and platform safety purposes. It is not used for advertising or personalisation and is not displayed to other users.

Date of birth may be processed by trusted service providers that help us operate, secure and maintain the platform.

### Account and Authentication Information

When you create or access an account, we may collect information required to identify and manage your account.

Fitly may use trusted third-party providers such as Firebase Authentication, Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In to support account creation and login.

### Usage, Device and Diagnostic Information

We may collect technical and usage information, including:

* Device type
* Operating system
* App version
* IP address
* General app usage activity
* Crash reports
* Diagnostic information
* Performance data
* Analytics information

This information helps us improve app performance, fix bugs, maintain security and understand how users interact with Fitly.

### Payment Information

Fitly is currently free for customers and trainers unless otherwise stated in the app.

If paid features, subscriptions or trainer listing fees are enabled in the future, payment information may be processed by trusted third-party payment providers such as Stripe, Apple or Google.

Fitly does not store full credit card details directly on its own servers.

## 3. How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

* Directly from users when creating an account, updating a profile or contacting support
* Through profile forms, trainer listings, uploaded images and in-app messages
* Automatically through app usage logs, analytics tools, crash reporting and security systems
* Through third-party service providers that support app functionality, authentication, analytics, storage, messaging, payments or security

## 4. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

* Provide and operate the Fitly platform
* Create and manage user accounts
* Verify that users meet the minimum age requirement
* Display trainer profiles to customers
* Allow customers and trainers to contact each other through the app
* Help customers browse, compare and discover trainers
* Help trainers manage their profile and enquiries
* Improve app functionality, design, performance and user experience
* Monitor, prevent and respond to misuse, fraud, spam, abuse or security issues
* Provide customer support
* Send important service-related communications
* Comply with legal obligations
* Enforce our Terms of Service and platform policies

## 5. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information in the following circumstances:

### With Other Users

Trainer profile information may be visible to customers using Fitly. This may include profile photos, business details, experience, services, prices, training locations, availability, qualifications, insurance status if provided, and other profile information entered by the trainer.

Customers may share information with trainers when contacting them through the app.

Date of birth is not displayed to other users.

### With Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers that help us operate Fitly. These may include providers for:

* Cloud hosting and database storage
* Authentication
* Analytics
* Crash reporting
* Payment processing, if paid features are enabled
* Email or customer support tools
* Security and fraud prevention
* App performance monitoring

These providers may process or store information in Australia or other jurisdictions where they operate.

### With Legal Authorities

We may disclose information where required by law, regulation, court order, government request or where necessary to protect our legal rights, users, platform, safety or security.

### Business Transfers

If Fitly is involved in a merger, acquisition, sale, restructure or transfer of business assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable privacy obligations.

We do not sell personal information to unrelated third parties.

## 6. Third-Party Services

Fitly may use trusted third-party services such as Firebase, Google services, Apple services, Stripe, analytics providers, crash reporting tools and cloud infrastructure providers.

These services may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

We use these services to support core app functions such as authentication, data storage, messaging, analytics, app stability, payments where applicable and security.

## 7. Data Storage and Overseas Processing

Fitly uses secure cloud infrastructure and third-party service providers to operate the platform.

Your information may be stored or processed in Australia or in other countries where our service providers operate.

Where information is processed outside Australia, we take reasonable steps to use reputable providers and protect personal information in accordance with applicable privacy obligations.

## 8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure.

Security measures may include:

* Secure cloud infrastructure
* Access controls
* Authentication protections
* Encryption in transit where supported
* Restricted administrative access
* Monitoring for errors, crashes and suspicious activity
* Regular review of app functionality, permissions and security settings

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide Fitly, manage accounts, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent misuse and enforce our policies.

After account deletion or termination, some information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, backup, compliance or dispute resolution purposes.

Where information is no longer needed, we will take reasonable steps to delete, de-identify or securely manage it.

## 10. Analytics, Device Identifiers and Tracking Technologies

Fitly may use analytics, crash reporting, device identifiers and similar technologies to understand app usage, improve performance, fix errors and enhance user experience.

These technologies may collect information such as device type, app version, operating system, crash logs, usage events and general interaction data.

Fitly does not use this information to sell personal data to advertisers.

If Fitly introduces advertising or tracking that requires additional consent, we will update this Privacy Policy and any required app permissions or disclosures.

## 11. Marketing Communications

We may use your contact details to send important service updates or, where permitted, marketing communications about Fitly.

You may opt out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions where available or by contacting us at [support@fitly.live](mailto:support@fitly.live).

We may still send important non-marketing communications related to your account, security, legal updates or app functionality.

## 12. User Rights

You may request to:

* Access personal information we hold about you
* Correct inaccurate or outdated information
* Update your profile information
* Delete your account
* Ask questions about how your information is handled
* Opt out of marketing communications

To make a privacy request, contact us at [support@fitly.live](mailto:support@fitly.live).

We may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

## 13. How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion through the Fitly app.

To delete your account:

1. Open the Fitly app.
2. Go to the Profile page.
3. Tap "Delete Account".
4. Confirm your choice when prompted.

Deleting your account will remove or disable your account profile from active use.

Some information may be retained where required for legal, compliance, security, backup, fraud prevention or dispute resolution purposes.

If you have trouble deleting your account, contact us at [support@fitly.live](mailto:support@fitly.live).

## 14. Children

Fitly is intended for users aged 18 and over.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a person under 18 has created an account, we may delete or restrict that account.

## 15. Health and Fitness Information

Fitly is a marketplace and discovery platform for personal training services.

Users may choose to share fitness goals, training preferences, mobility goals, weight loss goals or similar information with trainers or within the app.

Users should avoid sharing sensitive medical information through Fitly unless they are comfortable doing so.

Fitly is not a medical provider and does not provide medical advice.

Customers should seek appropriate medical or professional advice before beginning a fitness program if they have health concerns, injuries or medical conditions.
